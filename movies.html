Marvel Cinematic Universe Movies

Iron Man (2008)

Billionaire inventor Tony Stark builds a high-tech armored suit after being captured by terrorists, becoming the hero known as Iron Man and launching the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

The Incredible Hulk (2008)

On the run from the military, Bruce Banner searches for a cure while struggling to control the Hulk within him.

Iron Man 2 (2010)

With the world watching, Tony faces new enemies, a failing arc reactor, and the arrival of S.H.I.E.L.D.’s Black Widow.

Thor (2011)

Banished to Earth, Thor must prove himself worthy while his brother Loki schemes for power in Asgard.

Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)

During World War II, Steve Rogers transforms into Captain America after receiving a super-soldier serum, becoming a symbol of hope and courage.

The Avengers (2012)

Earth’s mightiest heroes unite to stop Loki’s invasion, proving the Avengers can work together against impossible odds.

Iron Man 3 (2013)

After the Battle of New York, Tony confronts anxiety, the Mandarin threat, and what it truly means to be Iron Man.

Thor: The Dark World (2013)

Thor faces a powerful new threat as ancient forces awaken, forcing him to make difficult choices to protect both Earth and Asgard while testing his loyalty, strength, and sense of duty.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014)

Steve uncovers a conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D. and faces the Winter Soldier, changing the MCU’s political landscape.

Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

A group of misfits from across the galaxy come together when a powerful object draws them into a conflict that could affect the fate of the universe.
