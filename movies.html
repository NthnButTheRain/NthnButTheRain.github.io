The Infinity Saga (Phases 1–3)

Infinity Saga movie list

Iron Man (2008)

Billionaire inventor Tony Stark builds a high-tech armored suit after being captured by terrorists, becoming the hero known as Iron Man and launching the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

The Incredible Hulk (2008)

On the run from the military, Bruce Banner searches for a cure while struggling to control the Hulk within him.

Iron Man 2 (2010)

With the world watching, Tony faces new enemies, a failing arc reactor, and the arrival of S.H.I.E.L.D.’s Black Widow.

Thor (2011)

Banished to Earth, Thor must prove himself worthy while his brother Loki schemes for power in Asgard.

Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)

During World War II, Steve Rogers transforms into Captain America after receiving a super-soldier serum, becoming a symbol of hope and courage.

The Avengers (2012)

Earth’s mightiest heroes unite to stop Loki’s invasion, proving the Avengers can work together against impossible odds.

Iron Man 3 (2013)

After the Battle of New York, Tony confronts anxiety, the Mandarin threat, and what it truly means to be Iron Man.

Thor: The Dark World (2013)

Thor faces a powerful new threat as ancient forces awaken, forcing him to make difficult choices to protect both Earth and Asgard while testing his loyalty, strength, and sense of duty.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014)

Steve uncovers a conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D. and faces the Winter Soldier, changing the MCU’s political landscape.

Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

A group of misfits from across the galaxy come together when a powerful object draws them into a conflict that could affect the fate of the universe.

Avengers: Age of Ulron (2015)

Tony’s AI experiment backfires, creating Ultron and forcing the Avengers to fight for humanity’s future.

Ant-Man (2015)

Scott Lang becomes Ant-Man, learning to shrink and fight while pulling off a high-stakes heist against dangerous tech.

Captain America: Civil War (2016)

Political pressure and ideological conflict divide the Avengers, turning allies against one another as personal beliefs and loyalties are put to the test.

Doctor Strange (2016)

After a career-ending accident, surgeon Stephen Strange discovers mystic arts and becomes a guardian against dark threats.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017)

The Guardians unravel Peter Quill’s origins, face a godlike threat, and discover what family really means.

Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017)

Peter Parker returns home after a taste of hero life, eager to prove himself while facing a new threat that tests what being Spider-Man truly means.

Thor: Ragnarok (2017)

Stranded far from home, Thor must overcome unexpected challenges to return to Asgard and stop a powerful new threat.

Black Panther (2018)

T’Challa returns to Wakanda as king, but must confront a challenger whose plan could change the world forever.

Avengers: Infinity War (2018)

The Avengers and allies race to stop Thanos from collecting the Infinity Stones and wiping out half of all life.

Ant-Man and The Wasp (2018)

As Scott Lang faces the consequences of his choices, he teams up with the Wasp on a mission that tests his priorities as both a hero and a father.

Captain Marvel (2019)

Carol Danvers uncovers her past and immense powers during a cosmic war, becoming one of the MCU’s strongest heroes.

Avengers: Endgame (2019)

Following a universe-shattering defeat, the remaining Avengers come together for one last stand in Marvel's conclusion to the Infinity Saga.

Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019)

This epilogue to the Infinity Saga follows Peter Parker on a school trip to Europe as new threats emerge in a world reshaped by the events of Avengers: Endgame.
