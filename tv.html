Marvel Cinematic Universe TV Shows

* Disney+ Exclusive Series

Wandavision (2021) | 1 Season

Wanda Maximoff and Vision appear to live an ideal suburban life, but shifting sitcom eras and unsettling glitches reveal a deeper reality.

The Falcon and The Winter Soldier (2021) | 1 Season

Sam Wilson and Bucky Barnes are forced into an uneasy partnership as new global tensions rise in the aftermath of the Blip. While a radical movement challenges the world’s fragile stability, the government introduces its own version of Captain America — pushing Sam to confront what the shield truly represents.

Loki (2021-2023) | 2 Seasons

After escaping with the Tesseract, Loki is captured by the Time Variance Authority and forced to confront variants of himself across branching timelines. His journey reshapes the multiverse and destabilizes reality itself.

What If…? (2021-2024) | 3 Seasons

The Watcher explores alternate realities where pivotal MCU moments unfold differently. Each episode reimagines heroes and villains in unexpected roles across the multiverse.

Hawkeye (2021) | 1 Season

Clint Barton’s past as Ronin resurfaces, pulling him into a new conflict during the holidays. Teaming up with skilled archer Kate Bishop, he must confront old enemies while passing the torch to the next generation.

Moon Knight (2022) | 1 Season

Steven Grant begins experiencing blackouts and disturbing visions that reveal he may not be alone in his own mind. As ancient forces tied to Egyptian mythology emerge, he is drawn into a dangerous conflict that blurs the line between reality and the supernatural.

Ms. Marvel (2022) | 1 Season

Kamala Khan, a superhero-obsessed teenager from Jersey City, suddenly gains extraordinary abilities of her own. As she learns to control her powers, she must balance family, friendships, and school while discovering what it truly means to be a hero.

I Am Groot (2022-2023) | 2 Seasons

A collection of short animated adventures following Baby Groot as he explores the universe, gets into tiny trouble, and learns big lessons in his own adorable way.

She-Hulk: Attorney At Law (2022) | 1 Season

Attorney Jennifer Walters gains Hulk-like powers while trying to maintain her career and personal life. The series blends superhero action with comedy as she navigates a world full of superhuman legal chaos.

Werewolf By Night (2022) | Special Presentation

A secret group of monster hunters gathers at a remote estate to compete for a powerful relic after their leader’s death. As tensions rise and danger closes in, one of them hides a terrifying secret that could change the hunt—and everyone in it.

The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special (2022) | Special Presentation

Determined to give Peter Quill the perfect Christmas, the Guardians travel to Earth in search of a legendary gift. Their plan quickly spirals into a chaotic and heartfelt holiday adventure filled with mischief and unexpected surprises.

Secret Invasion (2023) | 1 Season

Nick Fury uncovers a covert Skrull infiltration that has embedded itself within Earth’s most powerful institutions. Trust fractures as paranoia and betrayal threaten global stability.

Echo (2024) | 1 Season

Maya Lopez returns to her hometown to confront her violent past and her connection to Wilson Fisk. Rooted in crime drama, the series explores identity, family, and redemption.

X-Men '97 (2024-) | 1 Season

After the loss of their mentor, the X-Men struggle to keep their team — and their purpose — together in a world that still fears and distrusts mutants. As new threats rise and old tensions return, the group must fight to protect both mutantkind and the people who may never accept them.

Agatha All Along (2024) | 1 Season

After losing her powers, Agatha Harkness is freed from a twisted spell by a mysterious teen who asks her to guide him down the legendary Witches’ Road — a deadly magical trial that promises to grant a witch what they’re missing. As they gather a coven and begin the journey, dark forces await along the path.

Your Friendly Neighborhood Spider-Man (2025-) | 1 Season

This animated series reimagines Peter Parker’s early days as Spider-Man, exploring his journey to becoming a hero in a fresh take on the classic origin story. As he balances school, friendships, and newfound responsibilities, Peter begins carving out his place in the Marvel universe.

Daredevil: Born Again (2025-) | 1 Season

Matt Murdock continues his fight for justice as Daredevil while Wilson Fisk rises to new political power. Their renewed conflict reshapes the street-level world of the MCU.

Ironheart (2025) | 1 Season

After returning home to Chicago, genius inventor Riri Williams builds her own advanced armor and is pulled into a dangerous conflict where technology collides with magic, drawing her into the orbit of the mysterious Parker Robbins (The Hood).

Eyes of Wakanda (2025) | 1 Season

Marvel Animation follows Wakandan warriors across different eras as they travel the world on dangerous missions to recover stolen vibranium artifacts—protecting Wakanda’s secrets and stopping powerful enemies from turning its technology into a weapon.

Marvel Zombies (2025) | 1 Season

After the Avengers are overwhelmed by a zombie plague, a small group of survivors races across a ruined world to uncover a way to stop the super-powered undead - risking everything to save what’s left.

Wonder Man (2026) | 1 Season

Aspiring actor Simon Williams is trying to break into Hollywood when a chance connection with Trevor Slattery pulls him into a high-stakes opportunity: a legendary director is remaking the in-universe superhero film “Wonder Man.” As the two chase the role of a lifetime, the series peeks behind the curtain of fame, ambition, and the entertainment industry.
